Dallas 1 Test Checklist

1. Room Readiness
Physical room and cable readiness
Verify display is intact, USB-C cable is accessible, no loose/disconnected cables are visible, and the room appears ready for use.
{{RADIOS:room-ready}}
Evoko Naso panel check
Verify the Naso panel is powered, responsive, shows Dallas 1, and displays room availability.
{{RADIOS:naso-ready}}
2. Display Control
Display ON/OFF via Impera Uniform 8U
Turn display OFF, wait 10 seconds, then turn display ON. Confirm reliable response and no stuck “No Signal” state.
{{RADIOS:display-on-off}}
3. Local Presentation
USB-C local presentation
Connect laptop to EasyConnect MPX250 USB-C. Confirm content appears on display with correct aspect ratio and no flicker.
{{RADIOS:local-video}}
USB-C charging
Confirm connected laptop indicates it is charging from the MPX250 USB-C connection.
{{RADIOS:usb-charge}}
4. USB Peripheral Access
Camera appears on laptop
Open Teams/Zoom device settings and confirm the Vidi250 camera is available as a selectable camera.
{{RADIOS:camera-enumeration}}
Microphone and speaker appear on laptop
Confirm room microphone and speaker playback devices are available in the UC client audio settings.
{{RADIOS:audio-enumeration}}
5. Camera, Microphone, and Speaker
Camera preview
Open camera preview in UC client. Confirm live image, reasonable framing, no freeze, and acceptable latency.
{{RADIOS:camera-preview}}
Microphone pickup
Speak from seated positions and confirm audio meter activity with clean, intelligible pickup.
{{RADIOS:microphone}}
Speaker playback
Play test audio from laptop. Confirm clear playback through room speakers with no distortion or crackling.
{{RADIOS:speaker-playback}}
6. BYOM Meeting Workflow
Teams BYOM call
Join a Teams test meeting from laptop. Confirm far end sees room camera, hears room microphone, and local room hears far-end audio.
{{RADIOS:byom-call}}
Content share during call
Share content from the laptop during the active Teams meeting. Confirm content is visible locally and to the far end.
{{RADIOS:content-share-call}}
7. Disconnect Recovery
Return to ready state
Disconnect USB-C from laptop. Confirm display and room system return to idle/default state without frozen content.
{{RADIOS:disconnect-recovery}}
